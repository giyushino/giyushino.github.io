About Me

I'm Allan, a frosh studying applied mathematics, but I am looking to tack on another major. I'm currently pretty interested in machine learning, specifically the fields of computer vision and LMMs. However, I know myself well enough to know that my interests can change at the drop of a hat. While I'm not exactly sure what I'll be doing in the future, I am pretty keen on going to graduate school and conducting research related to machine learning.

Currently, I'm working as a research assistant at BigML @ UCLA, where I'm currently helping a graduate student study where and why multimodal models fail at spatial reasoning tasks. It has been an extremely fulfilling experience and I have learned a lot. When I'm not working on research, I enjoy reading and creating my own personal projects. Since I only started learning to code a few months ago, I've been trying to code as much as possible. While I have mostly been working with Python and machine learning, I hope to expand my skillset to C++ (maybe gamedev?).

My current project involves testing different methods of finetuning an LLM to become an optimal math agent. I have been experimenting and studying how datasets, model architecture, compute power, prompting, low rank adaption, and other factors can affect the model's performance.

Purpose

What are humans apart from our memories? Strip them away from us and what do we become? With this site, I hope to keep a record of my thought process, works, and life in general. Even though I have only been in college for a few months, I've come to understand how fast paced life can be, and how fleeting moments are. Without any pictures or written records, I find myself wondering how exactly I felt at certain points my life, or what exactly I did. I feel dissapointed by the fact that my truimphs, failures, and thoughts are essentially lost to time due to my negligence.
